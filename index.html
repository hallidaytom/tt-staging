About Aaron

A Coach Built For Real Change

Aaron Montague is a coach and advisor working with individuals and organisations navigating change, growth and transition. He brings over 12 years of experience in project management and change-focused roles within large, complex organisations in Australia and the UK.

This background shapes a grounded, no-nonsense coaching style, informed by a strong understanding of systems, governance and the realities of high-pressure environments. His work integrates strategy, mindset and behaviour — helping clients move from insight to action in a way that feels aligned, practical and sustainable.
