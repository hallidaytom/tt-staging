About Asset150

Three directors. Decades of experience. One focused capability.

Asset150 is led by three directors with deep experience across asset-intensive industries including utilities, transportation and defence.

Collectively, the leadership team brings decades of experience in strategy, architecture and delivery of complex asset management and operational systems. Their background spans enterprise platforms such as SAP, GIS, and operational technologies, with a strong focus on delivering solutions that work in real-world environments.

"Combining strategic insight, strong governance and practical delivery capability — the Asset150 approach is built on experience earned in the field, not the boardroom."

The directors have held senior roles across major transformation programs, leading multidisciplinary teams and working closely with executives to align technology with operational outcomes. This experience underpins Asset150's approach — combining strategic insight, strong governance and practical delivery capability.

Asset150 was established to bring this experience together into a focused capability — helping organisations design, implement and operate asset management solutions that are adopted, sustained and deliver measurable value.
