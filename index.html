About

Aaron Montague

Aaron Montague is a certified Enneagram practitioner, mindset coach, and organisational consultant based in Australia. Since 2011, he has worked with hundreds of individuals and businesses to create lasting, transformative change.

His approach sits at the intersection of deep psychological insight and practical business acumen. Aaron believes that true performance — in sport, leadership, or life — begins with radical self-knowledge.

Whether in one-on-one coaching sessions, corporate workshops, or multi-day Enneagram intensives, Aaron creates the conditions for people to step fully into who they are — and who they can become.
