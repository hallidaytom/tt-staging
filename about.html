About Asset150

We exist to raise the standard of asset management in Australia.

Asset150 is a specialist consultancy focused exclusively on asset-intensive industries where operational performance is non-negotiable.

Asset150 was founded on a conviction that most asset management engagements deliver less than they promise — not because the people aren't capable, but because strategy is separated from execution, and theory is separated from operations.

We built this firm differently. Our team brings together senior practitioners — people who have led real programs, operated in live environments, and understood what it actually takes to get asset management systems to work in the field, not just on paper.

We serve utilities, rail and defence organisations because those are the sectors where getting asset management right genuinely matters — where asset failures cascade into community, safety and regulatory consequences.

Our approach integrates strategy, system architecture, program leadership and implementation delivery into a coherent whole. We don't hand over a report and walk away. We stay in the problem until the outcome is real.

Asset150's name reflects our commitment to the ISO 55001 standard — the international benchmark for asset management systems. It's not a credential we wear. It's the baseline we work from.
