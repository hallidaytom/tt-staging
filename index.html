Personal Coaching FAQs

Aaron’s coaching is direct, practical and grounded in genuine insight about who you are. Sessions are one-on-one and centred on your goals and challenges. Rather than generic frameworks or motivational scripts, Aaron uses the Enneagram — one of the most sophisticated personality systems available — combined with Law of Attraction principles to give you both self-knowledge and a clear sense of direction. Most clients describe it as the most useful conversation they’ve had about themselves.

The Enneagram is a personality typology system that maps nine distinct types, each defined by core motivations, fears and behavioural patterns. Unlike surface-level assessments, the Enneagram gets at the why behind how you think, feel and act — particularly under stress or pressure. Aaron uses the Enneagram as a foundation because it creates genuine self-awareness that translates into practical, lasting change. It is not used as a label, but as a lens through which to understand yourself and grow.

Sessions are delivered both in person and via video call, depending on your location and preference. Aaron works with clients locally and remotely across Australia. All programs are structured to work equally well in either format.

Program length varies by tier. The Initial Enneagram Profiling Session is a single 90-minute deep-dive. The Essentials Program includes multiple sessions over a structured period, typically spread across 6–8 weeks. The Essentials Plus Program extends this further. Specific session numbers and timelines are confirmed when you book. Aaron’s approach ensures momentum is maintained between sessions with summaries and support materials.

No — and in fact, arriving without a fixed idea of your type is often better. Aaron uses a skilled profiling process to identify your type through conversation and observation, rather than relying solely on self-report questionnaires. Life In My Hands coaching takes a holistic view of life — covering relationships, career and purpose, health and wellbeing, and financial clarity. These areas are addressed together, because change in one area inevitably affects the others. Rather than siloing your challenges, Aaron works with you to understand the underlying patterns driving your experience across all of them.

Aaron is the sole practitioner — every client works directly with him from start to finish. There are no juniors, no handoffs and no template approaches. The depth of the Enneagram work, combined with Aaron’s corporate and change management background, means clients get both psychological depth and practical, grounded guidance. It’s a rare combination.

There’s no pressure and no obligation in that first conversation.

Workshop FAQs

The Enneagram workshops are designed for anyone with a genuine interest in understanding themselves and others more deeply — whether for personal growth, professional development or to build skills as a practitioner or coach. The goal-setting workshop is open to anyone ready to approach their goals with more clarity and intention. Participants 17 and over are welcome.

No prior knowledge is required for EP1. It is the entry point into the Enneagram education pathway and is fully self-contained. You will leave with a thorough understanding of all nine personality types, a copy of The Wisdom of the Enneagram by Riso & Hudson, and a set of Enneagram Personality Cards.

Physiognomy is the practice of reading personality traits from physical characteristics and non-verbal cues — facial structure, posture, gestures and expression. In the context of Enneagram profiling, it allows a skilled practitioner to identify someone’s type through observation, rather than relying solely on what a person says about themselves. This makes profiling more accurate and more useful. EP2 teaches this skill to participants who have completed EP1.
