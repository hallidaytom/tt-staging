About Aaron

A grounded,
no-nonsense
approach.

Aaron Montague is a coach and advisor working with individuals and organisations navigating change, growth and transition. He brings over 12 years' experience in project management and change-focused roles within large, complex organisations in Australia and the UK.

This background shapes a coaching style informed by a strong understanding of systems, governance and the realities of high-pressure environments. His work integrates strategy, mindset and behaviour — helping clients move from insight to action in a way that feels aligned, practical and sustainable.

Aaron is the sole practitioner delivering Life In My Hands services, ensuring consistency, accountability and continuity across every engagement.
